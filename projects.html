My Projects

Traffic Light Using Arduino

Overview: A simulation of a real-world traffic light control system using Arduino and LEDs.

Details: Programmed an Arduino Uno to control red, yellow, and green LEDs in a proper traffic sequence with delay timers. This project helped understand microcontroller logic, timing circuits, and basic electronics.

Skills Used: Arduino programming, LED circuit design, logic building.

Car Theft Detection & Toll Tax System

Overview: Combined two modules – theft alert and automatic toll payment – into a single Arduino-based system.

Details: Implemented sensors to detect unauthorized car movement and trigger a buzzer alarm. Also used RFID tags and readers to simulate auto toll deduction when passing through a toll gate. Aimed to increase vehicle safety and simplify toll transactions.

Skills Used: Arduino, RFID, buzzer systems, sensor integration, prototyping.
